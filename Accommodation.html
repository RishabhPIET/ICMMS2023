    • How To Reach

    Poornima Institute of Engineering and Technology Collage, ISI-6, RIICO Institutional Area, Sitapura,
    Jaipur, Rajasthan 302022 is situated in Northern India at a distance of around 270 km south of Delhi.

    By Road:

    From National Highway 8: Which connects Jaipur to Delhi, Ajmer, Udaipur, Ahmadabad, Vadodara, Mumbai,
    one can reach to Sindhi Camp Bus stand. From Sindhi camp which is 22 kms away from Poornima Institute
    of Engineering and Technology Collage one can take bus, prepaid taxi, Ola /Uber /Taxi for You cabs or an
    Auto to reach PIET campus.

    From National Highway 11: Which connects Agra-Jaipur-Bikaner one can reach to Sindhi Camp Bus stand.
    From Sindhi camp which is 22 kms away from Poornima Institute of Engineering and Technology Collage
    campus one can take bus, prepaid taxi, Ola/Uber/Taxi for You cabs or Auto to reach PIET campus.

    From National Highway 12: Which connects Jabalpur-Bhopal-Khilchipur-Aklera-Jhalawar-Kota-Bundi-Devli-Tonk-Jaipur
    one can reach to Sindhi Camp Bus stand. From Sindhi camp which is 22 kms away from Poornima Institute
    of Engineering Collage and Technology Collage campus one can take bus, prepaid taxi, Ola/ Uber/ Taxi or an
    Auto to reach PIET campus.

    By Train:

    Indian railways connect Jaipur to almost all major cities of the country. There are three stops close to Poornima
    Institute of Engineering Collage:one Durgapura, about 15 Kms from PCE, another at Ghandhi Nagar railway station,
    Third is main railway station is about 23 kms from MNIT, one can take bus, prepaid taxi, Ola/ Uber/ Taxi or
    an Auto to reach PIET campus.

    By Air:

    Delhi International Airport is closest if direct flight to Jaipur is not available. Poornima Institute of Engineering
    Collage is roughly 12 kilometers from Sanganer airport, Jaipur, from there one can Take, prepaid taxi, Ola /
    Uber/ Taxi cabs or an Auto to reach PIET campus.
