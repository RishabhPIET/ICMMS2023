Registration Guideline

Registration Process

  • It is mandatory that at least one of the authors of each accepted paper shall Register and present the paper in the technical session. All accepted papers will be considered for presentation only after the payment of registration fee.
  • Participants should send the registration form in specified format along with the registration fee.
  • For online payment please visit conference website.
  • Selected papers will be published in quality Journals like AIP, Scopus, Springer etc

INSTRUCTIONS

  • For each accepted paper, at least one of the authors must register for the conference.
  • One registration in the Author’s Registration Category is mandatory for
  • Presentation of the paper in the conference
  • Inclusion of paper in the conference proceedings.
  • If one of the authors of the paper has registered in author’s registration category then all other authors of the paper may register under co-authors’ registration category if they wish to attend the conference.
  • Per person registration fee structure is as follows –

Refund Policy

If a registrant is unable to attend an event for any reason, they may substitute, by arrangement with the registrar, someone else of Co-authors or from the same institute/organization.
Written requests for cancellations must be sent to Conference Secretary ( months before the conference), and 30 USD will be charged, (Two months before the conference), and 30% processing fee is required, (One months before the conference), and 50% of payment will be charged.
Cancellations received (Less than one month before the conference), will NOT be dismissed and registration fees will not be refunded.
