The conference revolves around bringing mathematics, engineering, technology, and sciences research areas synchronically on an indivisible platform. This conference is an opportunity for collaborative research from various fields to share research findings and latest ideas. The main intention of this conference is to integrate interdisciplinary inquiry to deliver the best applications. Attending this conference will help participants sharpen their skills and refine their ideas as well as approaches by meeting with their peers and counterparts. They will also have the chance to meet experts and high-level delegates, along with share ideas and research findings from past and present. Participants can build new networks to meet different personalities and learn about the latest tools available. The conference language is English. Original and unpublished works are encouraged for presentation in the conference. The thrust areas include (but are not limited to) the following;

TRACK 1: NUMBER THEORY & GENERAL ALGEBRAIC SYSTEMS
  • Fuzzy Logics
  • Field Theory and Polynomials
  • Commutative Algebra
  • Algebraic Geometry
  • Linear and Multilinear Algebra; Matrix Theory
  • Associative Rings and Algebras
  • Non-associative Rings and Algebras
  • Category Theory; Homological Algebra
  • Topological Groups, Lie Groups
TRACK 2: MATHEMATICAL ANALYSIS

  • Special Functions
  • Approximations and Expansion
  • Integral Transforms, Operational Calculus

TRACK 4: OPERATIONS RESEARCH
